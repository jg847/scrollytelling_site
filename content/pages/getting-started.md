This page exists so the content repository has at least one generated standard-layout slug.

## What this page demonstrates

The standard layout is the long-form scrollytelling surface. It keeps the main content in a readable column, lets supporting visuals stay in view while the text moves, and gives the story room to unfold as one continuous descent.

## How to use the site

Use the homepage and ocean zone pages for the main descent experience. Use standard pages like this one for setup notes, glossaries, references, and other material that benefits from a continuous reading flow.

For broader ocean background, visit [NOAA Ocean Service](https://oceanservice.noaa.gov/).

## What comes next

As the site grows, this layout will carry both support content and the primary storytelling flow, so the descent reads like one continuous animated narrative instead of a sequence of separate decks.
